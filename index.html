This web presents a demo about predicting the game churn rate of each group under different gameplay parameters in the offline manner. Here the gameplay is extraced from a real world game. There are three stages in this gameplay. At the first two stages agents cooperate or competitive with others to pick up a key at maximum and carry it until the end of this stage. Agents carrying a key at the end of this stage have the right to enter the next stage. Agent carrying a key will be marked to notify others. The agents who enter the third stage can pick up lots rewawrds for their success.
A game designer can select parameters of the gameplay, then a video of the game under these parameters is shown and the policy used here is trained by RL which is time-consuming.
